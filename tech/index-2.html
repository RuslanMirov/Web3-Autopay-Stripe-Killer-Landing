// core principles

Built different.
By design.

Every principle is a deliberate choice to eliminate the attack surface that plagues today's Web3 payment flows.

01
No Unlimited Approve
Tokens live inside a smart wallet — never approved to external contracts. Your funds, your rules.
02
No Unlimited Deposit
The wallet holds only what you put in. Services claim specific amounts — never open-ended access.
03
No Private Key Trust
Claims are authorized by on-chain subscription state, not by whoever holds a signing key.
04
Contract-Based Automation
The ERC-4337 EntryPoint enforces timing, caps, and whitelists — fully on-chain, fully auditable.
05
Real Auto-Pay
Services pull payment every 30 days without any user interaction. Truly set-and-forget subscriptions.
06
Simple and Safe UX
One deposit into your smart wallet and you're done. Security and simplicity don't have to trade off.
// how it works

From deposit to
auto-pay in minutes.

01

Deploy your smart wallet

A lightweight ERC-4337 wallet is created for you — fully self-custodied, no third party ever touches your keys.

02

Deposit once

Fund your wallet with the tokens you want to use for subscriptions. That's the only action you'll ever take.

03

Subscribe to a service

On-chain subscription state is created: amount, interval, and the service's whitelisted address. All capped. All enforced.

04

Let the contract do the rest

Every billing cycle, the EntryPoint validates and executes the claim. No signatures needed. No wallet popups. No trust.

SubscriptionWallet.sol
// User deposits once
deposit(100 USDC);

// Subscribe to a service
subscribe({
  service: 0xABC…,
  amount: 10 USDC,
  interval: 30 days,
});

// 30 days later — no action needed
// EntryPoint auto-executes:
validateAndClaim(subId);
// → verifies timing ✓
// → verifies cap ✓
// → verifies whitelist ✓
// ✓ Payment settled on-chain
